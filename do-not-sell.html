California residents have the right under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) to opt out of the sale of their personal information. This page explains your rights, what information we collect and share, and how to submit your opt-out request.

1. Your Rights Under CCPA

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you specific rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources from which it was collected, the purposes for collection and use, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request that we delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions permitted by law.
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale: You have the right to direct us to not sell your personal information to third parties. This page provides the mechanism to exercise that right.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights. We will not deny you services, charge you different prices, or provide a different level of service because you exercised a privacy right.

Under the CCPA, "sale" includes sharing personal information with third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration. As a lead generation referral service, sharing your information with our appliance repair partner network may constitute a "sale" under this definition.

2. What We Collect and Share

When you use ApplianceRescueNow.com to request appliance repair quotes, we collect and may share the following categories of personal information with third-party appliance repair companies and marketing partners:

  • Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing or service address.
  • Service Request Details: Appliance type, brand, model, age, and description of the problem requiring repair.
  • Property Information: Whether you own or rent, property type, and ZIP code or service area.
  • Internet or Network Activity: IP address, browser type, pages visited on our website, and referring website URLs.
  • Geolocation Data: General location derived from your IP address or the service address you provide.
  • Inferences: Inferences drawn from the above data to help match you with suitable appliance repair professionals.

We share this information primarily with appliance repair service providers, home services companies, and marketing partners in our referral network. These third parties may use the information to contact you with quotes, promotions, and related offers.

4. After You Submit

Once your opt-out request is received and verified, we will take the following steps:

  • We will stop selling your personal information to third-party appliance repair companies and marketing partners going forward.
  • We will notify any third parties to whom we sold your information in the 90 days prior to your request, directing them to honor your opt-out where required by law.
  • We will send a confirmation to the email address you provided once your request has been processed, within 15 business days.
  • Your opt-out preference will be stored for at least 12 months before we may ask you to reauthorize the sale of your information.

Please note that opting out of the sale of your information does not delete your information from our systems. To request deletion, please contact us separately at hello@appliancerescuenow.com. Additionally, opting out does not affect our ability to use your information for internal operations or to fulfill a service request you have already submitted.

If you have already received contacts from appliance repair companies as a result of a prior service request, those companies may have their own independent databases. You should contact each company separately to opt out of their communications.
