Common Questions

How quickly can I get a technician out?+
Same-day service is available in most areas. When you call, we match you with the nearest available local technician — often within hours of your call.
Is there any obligation when I call?+
None at all. A technician will diagnose the issue and give you a clear quote before any work begins. You decide whether to move forward — zero pressure.
What appliances do you cover?+
We connect you with technicians who repair all major household appliances including refrigerators, washers, dryers, ovens, stoves, ranges, dishwashers, freezers, ice makers, garbage disposals, wine coolers, and more.
Do you repair all brands?+
Local technicians in our network service most major brands including Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, GE, Frigidaire, Maytag, Bosch, KitchenAid, Kenmore, and more. Confirm brand availability when you call.
How much does appliance repair typically cost?+
Pricing depends on the appliance, the severity of the problem, parts needed, and your location. Your technician provides an exact quote on-site before any work starts — no hidden fees.
Is it better to repair or replace my appliance?+
A local technician can diagnose the issue and help you understand your options. As a general rule, if the repair cost is less than 50% of the replacement cost and the appliance has useful life remaining, repair is usually the better value.
